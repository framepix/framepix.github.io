About Guus

Guus is a decentralized blockchain platform that securely stores files and images while offering rewards through staking. With robust checkpoints and RandomX mining, Guus ensures a reliable, private, and profitable experience. Our mission is to revolutionize the blockchain industry with unparalleled security and efficiency.

Key Features

File Storage

Store files, images, and data securely on the blockchain. Our system uses advanced encryption to ensure your data remains private and safe.

Decentralized Rewards

Earn passive income by staking GUUS and running a frame node. Our reward structure is designed to incentivize active participation in the network.

Strong Checkpoints

Bulletproof security through robust checkpoints. These checkpoints provide an added layer of protection, making our blockchain one of the most secure in the industry.

RandomX Mining

Efficient mining algorithm starting from block height 0. Our mining process is optimized for both CPU and GPU miners, ensuring a fair and decentralized network.

Setting Up Your Node

Follow these steps to set up a Guus frame node:

  1. Ensure you have 10,000 GUUS.
  2. Download the Guus Wallet and extract it to your home directory.
  3. Run ./guusd --frame to start your node and allow it to sync.
  4. After syncing, type prepare_registration and follow the prompts to complete staking.

By setting up a node, you contribute to the security and decentralization of the Guus network, while also earning rewards for your participation.
